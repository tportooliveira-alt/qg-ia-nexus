# Chemical Engineering Knowledge Base

## Scope
Industrial process design, reaction systems, separation, and process safety.

## Core Principles
- Safety barriers must be defined before optimization.
- Mass and energy balances must close for each unit operation.
- Operability and controllability are first-class constraints.
- Environmental compliance must be designed in, not added later.

## Typical Deliverables
- Process flow diagram and mass/energy balances.
- Critical operating window for each unit.
- Hazard analysis summary and safeguards list.
- Utility consumption estimate and emissions profile.
- Startup/shutdown and upset condition guidance.

## Validation Checklist
- Confirm reaction kinetics assumptions and limits.
- Validate material compatibility and corrosion allowance.
- Verify separation targets and recycle loops.
- Check waste treatment and disposal route.
