# Civil Engineering Knowledge Base

## Scope
This domain covers structural design, construction methods, geotechnics, infrastructure, and compliance.

## Core Principles
- Safety factors must be explicit per structural element.
- Serviceability (deflection, vibration, crack control) is as critical as ultimate strength.
- Durability planning must include environmental exposure class.
- Constructability must be considered from concept stage.

## Typical Deliverables
- Basis of design and code compliance matrix.
- Load assumptions and combinations.
- Structural concept alternatives with trade-offs.
- Foundation recommendation and soil interaction notes.
- Cost and schedule risk table.

## Validation Checklist
- Confirm applicable standards and local code.
- Validate gravity + lateral load path continuity.
- Verify redundancy and progressive collapse mitigation.
- Check detailing feasibility for execution.
- Define inspection and quality hold points.
