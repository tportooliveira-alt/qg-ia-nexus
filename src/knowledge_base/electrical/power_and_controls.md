# Electrical Engineering Knowledge Base

## Scope
Power systems, electronics, controls, automation, and embedded integration.

## Core Principles
- Design for electrical safety first, then performance.
- Protect every subsystem with layered protection.
- Define control modes and fail-safe behavior explicitly.
- Maintain EMC/EMI constraints from architecture stage.

## Typical Deliverables
- Single-line diagrams and control block diagrams.
- Power budget with transient analysis.
- Protection and grounding strategy.
- Control loop assumptions and tuning bounds.
- Commissioning and acceptance test plan.

## Validation Checklist
- Verify normal, degraded, and fault operation modes.
- Validate thermal limits and derating assumptions.
- Confirm sensor/actuator interfaces and timing.
- Check maintainability and diagnostics coverage.
